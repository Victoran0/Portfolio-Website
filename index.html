About

I am a self motivated and passionate individual who has perseverance and this made me go the self taught route, because I know I can hold on, complete each course, research and build projects without losing enthusiasm.

Since the day I started my first course on Web development, I haven’t look back for once. I find these CSS courses interesting because I’ve always been interested in finding out how things work on computers.
